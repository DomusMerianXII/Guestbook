About Us

As a travel-loving family, we enjoy discovering new places – especially those with character. That's why we designed our holiday apartment the way we would like to stay ourselves: a mix of old and new, with attention to detail and an eye for the unique. We want every guest to feel at home with us. Of course, it's not everyone's style. But we believe you can feel how much heart we've put into this home away from home. It’s important to us that our guests feel welcome – like visiting friends. That they find peace, feel inspired, and leave with beautiful memories. When we're not hosting, we like to explore the region, discover new cafés, walking trails or favorite spots we’re happy to share. Domus Merian XII is not a standardized apartment. It’s our small passion project – personal, cozy, and maybe a little different.
